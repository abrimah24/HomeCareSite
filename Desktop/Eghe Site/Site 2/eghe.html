    Our Cause

    EONO began as a multifaceted homecare consulting agency. We lend our expert advice to businesses seeking to break into the home healthcare field. Our services span from assisting in the processes of establishing new homecare, home health, and home nursing agency to assisting in training employees for these types of agencies in the areas of in-service training which include CPR and first aid training.

    Our Expectation Guarenteed

    While consulting has been our focus, our expert knowledge of policy and procedure in regards to home care services has led to the establishment of a homecare agency of our own. EONO strives to provide quality care and fulfill the individual needs of every client.

    Our Purpose

    Our agency is different because we hope to foster relationships that make our clients feel like they are part of a family. Not only do our clients establish a relationship with our homemakers but our managers and supervisors develop an ongoing relationship with each client to ensure that they feel comfortable and well taken care of.

SERVICES

EONO Homecare Clients have access to a variety of services including:

  • Light Housekeeping
  • Laundry
  • Companionship
  • Meal Prep
  • Medication Reminders
  • Running Errands
  • Personal Grooming

OBTAINING SERVICES:

To obtain services we assist in the referral process via the:

  • The Department on Aging
  • The Department of Rehabilitation Services
  • The Department of Human Services
  • We also accept private pay clients

    • CAREGIVER TRAINING AND QUALIFICATIONS

    All homecare staff:

  • Are subject to 4 background checks as stipulated by the State of Illinois
  • Undergo 12 hours of preservice training
  • Undergo 24 hours of in service training annually
  • Have a passion for providing care to those in need

    • SUPERVISION

    All clients:

  • Receive a welcome package upon joining the agency
  • Receive monthly well-being calls
  • Undergo a 90 day case conference to gauge the status of their care
  • Visited by a supervisor every six months to assess the homemakers performance

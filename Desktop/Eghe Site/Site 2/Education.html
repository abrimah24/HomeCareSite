CPR TRAINING



Overview

According to the American Heart Association, more than 350,000 cardiac arrests occur outside of the hospital each year. Having personnel trained in CPR can be be the difference between life and death for you, your employees, and your clients and customers. EONO Enterprises provides the most up to date training in basic life support and strives to train individuals and give them the confidence to save one life at a time. Our AHA approved instructors ensure that every student is adequately trained in CPR and provide skills that allow each student to confidently deliver aid in an emergency situation.

Specifications

Class

Classes run from 2 - 3 hours. Training consists of one day of instruction which includes the following:
  1. 1 hour instructional video
  2. 30 minutes of simulated skills testing
  3. 20 minute 25 question assessment
  4. Certification sent via email
